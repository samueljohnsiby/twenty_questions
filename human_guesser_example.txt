Game Flow

1. Human: Is it a person?
AI: No.

2. Human: Is it a place?
AI: Yes.

3. Human: Is it a man-made place?
AI: No.

4. Human: Is it a natural feature?
AI: Yes.

5. Human: Is it located in the ocean?
AI: No.

6. Human: Is it located on land?
AI: Yes.

7. Human: Is it associated with a specific continent?
AI: Yes.

8. Human: Is it in Asia?
AI: Yes.

9. Human: Is it a desert?
AI: No.

10. Human: Is it a mountain?
AI: Yes.

11. Human: Is it part of the Himalayas?
AI: Yes.

12. Human: Is it a well-known mountain?
AI: Yes.

13. Human: Is it the tallest mountain in the world?
AI: Yes.

14. Human: Is it Mount Everest?
AI: Yes!
